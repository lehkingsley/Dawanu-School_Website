WELCOME TO ADAKLU DAWANU SALVATION ARMY BASIC SCHOOL!

Adaklu Dawanu Basic School was established in 1986 in town of Adaklu Dawanu. Our motto, 'Education for Excellence', reflects our passion for education and our commitment to excellence. We are proud to provide a supportive and inclusive learning environment for our students. The school was established to provide quality education to the children of the local community, with a focus on academic excellence, character development, and community service. Over the years, the school has experienced significant growth and expansion. From humble beginnings with a handful of students and teachers, the school has grown to become a thriving educational institution with a diverse student body and faculty.

Some notable milestones and achievements in the school's history include:

  • 1996: The school introduced its first junior high school program, marking a significant expansion of its academic offerings.
  • 1997: The school's football team won its first regional championship, beginning a tradition of athletic excellence.
  • 2015: The school introduced its first computer lab, providing students with access to cutting-edge technology.
  • 2016: The school celebrated its 30th anniversary with a grand ceremony attended by alumni, parents, and community leaders.
Today, Adaklu Dawanu School is a vibrant and dynamic educational community that is committed to providing a world-class education to its students. With a strong focus on academic excellence, character development, and community service, the school continues to inspire and empower its students to become leaders and change-makers in their communities. We are proud to be a part of the community and look forward to continuing to grow and evolve as a school.

School Facility

Adaklu Dawanu School boasts modern and well-maintained facilities that support a conducive learning environment. These facilities include spacious classrooms, a computer lab, library, science laboratories, sports facilities, toilet facilities, and a canteen. The school also has administrative facilities such as the Headteacher's Office and staff room. The school is secured and ensures accessibility for all students with features like ramps and adapted toilet facilities.
