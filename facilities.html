Adaklu Dawanu School is proud to offer a range of modern and well-maintained facilities that provide a supportive and inclusive learning environment for our students. Some of the key facilities in our school include:

Classrooms

We have three 3-unit classroom blocks which form a 'U' shape. Our classrooms are spacious, well-ventilated, and equipped with modern furniture and teaching aids. Each classroom is designed to promote interactive and engaging learning, with facilities such as whiteboards, markers, and educational charts.

Computer Lab

Our computer lab is equipped with modern computers and internet facilities, providing our students with access to a wide range of digital resources and learning tools. The lab is used for a variety of purposes, including computer studies, research, and online learning.

Library

Our library is a hub of learning and discovery, with a vast collection of books, journals, and other educational resources. The library is equipped with comfortable seating and study areas, making it an ideal place for students to read, research, and learn.

Sports Facilities

Our school has a range of sports facilities, including a football field, volleyball court, footballs and volleyballs. These facilities provide our students with opportunities to engage in physical activity, develop their sporting skills, and participate in inter-school competitions.

Toilet Facilities

Our school has modern and well-maintained toilet facilities, providing our students with a clean and hygienic environment. The facilities are regularly cleaned and maintained to ensure the health and well-being of our students.

Headteacher's Office

The Headteacher's Office is the administrative hub of our school, providing a welcoming and professional environment for parents, visitors, and students. The office is equipped with modern facilities and technology, enabling our administrative team to provide efficient and effective support to our school community.

Staff Room

Our staff room is a comfortable and welcoming space for our teachers, providing a place for relaxation, socializing, and professional development.
