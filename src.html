The Student Representative Council.

The Student Representative Council (SRC) is a group of students who represent the students in the school. The Student Council of Adaklu Dawanu School is a vibrant and dynamic group of student leaders who play a vital role in promoting the welfare and interests of the student body. The council is composed of dedicated and responsible students who are elected by their peers to represent their voices and concerns.

The Incumbent Leadership Team

At the helm of the Student Council is the Senior Prefect, Issah Yusif, who provides overall leadership and direction to the council. Issah is a respected and charismatic leader who has demonstrated exceptional leadership skills and a strong commitment to serving the student body.

Assisting the Senior Prefect is the Girls' Prefect, Cecelia Avorkliya, who is responsible for promoting the welfare and interests of the female students. Cecelia is a confident and compassionate leader who has earned the respect and admiration of her peers.

Other key members of the Student Council include:

  • Amewugah Agbenor, the Compound Overseer, who is responsible for maintaining the cleanliness and organization of the school compound.
  • Ahiati and Alice Akumani, the Office Girls, who provide administrative support to the council and assist with various tasks and projects.

    • Responsibilities and Initiatives

    The Student Council is responsible for organizing various events and activities that promote student engagement, inclusivity, and well-being. Some of the council's key initiatives include:

    • Organizing cultural events and celebrations that showcase the diversity and richness of the student body.
    • Providing support and resources for students who are struggling academically or personally.
    • Promoting environmental sustainability and conservation through initiatives such as recycling programs and clean-up campaigns.
    • Fostering a sense of community and belonging among students through social events and activities.
    The Student Council of Adaklu Dawanu School is a shining example of student leadership and initiative. Through their hard work and dedication, the council has created a positive and inclusive school culture that promotes student well-being, engagement, and success.
